🧘 HOW TO PLAY · CALM CURSOR DODGER

  • ✨ Move your mouse / finger vertically on the game canvas. The glowing orb follows your cursor Y position.
  • 🌀 Dodge dark crystals — they scroll from right to left. Each crystal has a random gap. Fly through the gap to survive.
  • 📈 Score increases each time you pass a crystal set. Speed rises with your distance — from 3 → 8 pixels/frame.
  • 💀 Game over if you touch any crystal or hit top/bottom edge. Click RESTART or tap canvas to begin a new run.
  • 🎯 The Flow Meter shows your relative vertical position (0% = top, 100% = bottom). Smooth drifting = high control.
